What is Silva CMS?

Silva is a free open source CMS (Content Management System) with a community focusing reliability through test driven development. A CMS choice can be hard and you need to look for a CMS which best fits the needs of your organization and users. As Silva has lots of features and expandability we hope Silva fulfills your needs the best and makes your choice easy. Silva CMS was made to make it easy and simple to create, structure and publish content for the web.
